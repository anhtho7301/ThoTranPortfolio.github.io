Marketing Analysis Report

Netflix's Global Operations
and Its Marketing Mix

This report examines Netflix's global operations, focusing on the challenges it faces in various countries and its marketing mix. It delves into two of the 4Ps: pricing and product. The pricing analysis highlights market-specific strategies and the impact of lowering subscription prices on market penetration and net profit. The product analysis emphasizes the importance of content inventory and cultural preferences, with "Squid Game" as a key example. The report also evaluates a promotional video for Netflix's 25th anniversary as an example of glocal promotion and summarizes recent operations in Vietnam. It includes an analysis of industry charts from Statista and concludes with a prediction that Netflix's net income will remain constant or decline over the next five years, noting the need for additional information to increase confidence in this prediction.
